Marquee Basics

Less is More

Your customers are speeding by at the speed of traffic. The message should be designed to catch your customer's attention and be easy to read.

Fully Extend the Pole

In most cases, extending the pole to it's full length is best. This keeps your arms low, making it easier to operate while reducing strain and fatigue.

Push Up, Drop Down

Letters and tracking are designed to allow the top of the letter to slip up and behind the tracking and then drop down into the bottom track.

Replace worn out suction cups

The number one cause of broken letters, tracking, and sign faces is a bad suction cup on your letter pole. Replace it every 12-18 months.
